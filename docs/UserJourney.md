# User Journey

## Recruiter entry
1. Recruiter lands on the portfolio entry point.
2. The AI persona introduces itself and explains the interaction model.
3. The recruiter chooses a starting path: learn about skills, projects, or ask questions.

## Conversational flow
1. Recruiter asks a question or selects a topic.
2. The AI responds with context-aware statements and follow-up prompts.
3. The recruiter can request deeper detail, project examples, or career narrative.

## Outcomes
- Recruiter gets a sense of core strengths and narrative quickly.
- Recruiter experiences the personality, voice, and technical story.
- Recruiter can probe for specific achievements, technologies, or team preferences.

## Future voice path
- AI persona response is also available via ElevenLabs audio output.
- Recruiter hears a spoken summary of projects, accomplishments, and technical experience.
- Recruiter transitions from text-first conversation to immersive audio-guided storytelling.
