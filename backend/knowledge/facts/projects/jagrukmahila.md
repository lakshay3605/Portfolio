# Jagruk Mahila

## Overview

Jagruk Mahila is an AI-powered legal awareness platform developed as part of an **ICSSR-sponsored research initiative**. The project aims to make legal information more accessible, understandable and easier to navigate, particularly for Muslim women seeking awareness about their legal rights.

Rather than replacing legal professionals, the platform uses AI to simplify complex legal information and improve accessibility for people who may not have easy access to legal guidance.

---

## Status

**Live — Completed and publicly available.**

**Website:** https://jagrukmahila.in

---

## Problem Statement

Many people are unaware of their legal rights or struggle to find reliable legal information in a simple and understandable format.

Legal resources are often scattered, difficult to interpret or written using complex legal terminology.

Jagruk Mahila was created to bridge this gap by providing an accessible platform that combines AI with verified legal awareness resources.

---

## What the Platform Does

The platform is designed to:

* Make legal information more accessible through AI-assisted guidance.
* Help users understand legal rights using clear and simple language.
* Provide a conversational AI chatbot for legal awareness.
* Support an ICSSR-backed initiative focused on increasing legal awareness.
* Combine academic research with a practical product that people can actually use.

## Engineering Challenges

One of the biggest challenges was ensuring that AI-generated responses remained useful while avoiding misleading legal advice.

This required thinking beyond prompt engineering and considering:

* User trust
* Response reliability
* Responsible AI usage
* Clear communication
* Appropriate limitations of AI systems

The project reinforced the importance of building AI that is not only intelligent but also dependable.
