# Business Intelligence Assistant

## Overview

An AI-powered business intelligence platform that transforms complex business data into meaningful insights using intelligent AI workflows.

## Status

Under development — actively being built, not yet fully shipped.

## What it does

- Helps teams turn business data into actionable insights
- Uses intelligent AI workflows rather than static dashboards alone
- Designed for real decision-making, not demo charts

## Technologies

- LLMs
- RAG
- FastAPI
- Agentic AI

## How Lakshay talks about this project

- Be clear it is still in progress.
- Emphasize the product goal: meaningful insights from complex data.
- Highlight end-to-end ownership of AI workflow design.
