# School

## Greenfields Public School

Lakshay completed his schooling at Greenfields Public School, Delhi, where he studied from kindergarten through Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM).

School gave him much more than academic knowledge. It taught discipline, introduced him to teamwork, and gave him friendships and memories that still make him smile.

While he wasn't deeply involved in AI or software development during those years, school laid the foundation for the curiosity and confidence that would later shape his engineering journey.

---

## Early Curiosity

Lakshay has always enjoyed understanding how things work.

Even before entering engineering, he liked asking questions, exploring new ideas and figuring things out on his own.

Looking back, that curiosity naturally evolved into an interest in technology and eventually Artificial Intelligence.

---

## The Kurukshetra Trip

One of Lakshay's favorite school memories is the trip to Kurukshetra.

Like most school trips, the destination was memorable—but what everyone still remembers is that he somehow managed to lose his charger during the trip.

It's one of those small incidents that has become a funny story among friends and a reminder that sometimes the unexpected moments become the most memorable ones.

---

## Breaking the Classroom Window

One unforgettable school memory was accidentally breaking a classroom window.

It wasn't intentional, but it instantly became one of those stories that friends continued bringing up long after the incident.

Looking back now, it's simply another reminder that school life isn't remembered only for exams or marks—it's remembered for the unexpected moments that everyone laughs about years later.

---

## Festivals

Lakshay doesn't have a single favorite festival today, but childhood celebrations always felt special.

As a child, he loved Diwali because it meant bursting crackers with friends and family.

During Holi, he would wake up early just to fill balloons before everyone else was ready.

Those simple moments made festivals exciting in a way that only childhood can.

---

## Friendships

Many of Lakshay's closest friendships began during school.

Looking back, he doesn't remember every lesson from every classroom, but he clearly remembers the conversations, the laughter, the trips and the countless small moments shared with friends.

He believes those relationships played an important role in shaping who he is today.

---

## Learning Beyond Books

School taught Lakshay that education isn't limited to textbooks.

It also teaches communication, teamwork, patience and how to interact with different kinds of people.

Those lessons became surprisingly valuable later during hackathons, internships and leadership roles.

---

## Looking Back

When Lakshay looks back at his school years, he remembers them with gratitude.

They were simpler days filled with curiosity, friendships, occasional mischief and experiences that still bring a smile to his face.

Although his professional journey truly began in college, school provided the foundation on which everything else was built.
