# Achievements

## National Hackathon Winner — Bid 2 Code

Winning the national-level Bid 2 Code Hackathon at JECRC Jaipur is one of the most significant milestones in Lakshay's journey.

The achievement wasn't meaningful simply because of the trophy. It was meaningful because it proved that consistent preparation, teamwork and execution could compete with talented students from across the country.

Working alongside teammates Sharjil Sharma, Lakshya Gupta and Sthal Pathak, Lakshay learned that good ideas alone are never enough. Success comes from communication, adaptability and delivering under pressure.

The experience gave him confidence that he belonged in highly competitive technical environments.

## AI Developer Intern at KVGAI

Securing an internship at KVGAI marked Lakshay's transition from building projects for learning to contributing to AI products with real-world expectations.

The internship exposed him to prompt engineering, LLM evaluation, AI workflows and product development.

More importantly, it taught him to think beyond code and focus on users, reliability and ownership.

It reinforced his belief that engineering is about solving problems rather than simply writing software.

---

## Leading Technical Communities

Lakshay has always enjoyed creating opportunities for others to learn.

Leading communities such as THINK AI and HackSphere gave him the opportunity to organize technical workshops, hackathons and collaborative events.

These experiences helped him grow as a communicator and leader while allowing him to contribute to the learning journey of other students.

For Lakshay, leadership has never been about titles—it has always been about creating impact.

---

## Building Real Projects

Throughout college, Lakshay deliberately focused on building projects that solved meaningful problems instead of creating projects solely to strengthen his resume.

Each project became an opportunity to learn new technologies, improve product thinking and understand user needs.

His portfolio reflects continuous improvement rather than isolated achievements.

---

## Learning Through Consistency

Lakshay considers consistency one of his biggest achievements.

Rather than relying on short bursts of motivation, he has consistently invested time in learning, building, participating in hackathons and exploring new technologies.

Many of the opportunities he has received are the result of that long-term consistency rather than a single breakthrough moment.

---

## Growing Confidence

One achievement that isn't reflected on a resume is confidence.

Earlier in college, Lakshay sometimes questioned whether he could compete with students from more established institutions.

Winning competitions, securing internships and leading communities gradually replaced that self-doubt with confidence built on experience.

Today, he believes confidence should come from preparation and consistent effort rather than assumptions.

---

## Recognition Through Responsibility

Lakshay values trust more than certificates.

Whenever teammates, mentors or organizers trusted him with important responsibilities, he considered that recognition just as meaningful as any award.

Being known as someone who can be relied upon is one of the achievements he values the most.

---

## Looking Forward

Lakshay doesn't see these achievements as destinations.

Instead, he sees them as milestones in a much longer journey.

His goal is to continue building AI products that create real impact, keep learning from every opportunity and become someone known for ownership, reliability and continuous growth.
