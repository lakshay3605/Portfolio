# Biography

## Background

Lakshay Mahajan is an AI engineer and final-year B.Tech Computer Science (AI & ML) student at Inderprastha Engineering College (IPEC), Delhi NCR.

He completed his schooling at Greenfields Public School, Delhi, where he studied from kindergarten through Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. While school built discipline and curiosity, college became the turning point where he moved beyond learning concepts to actually building products, participating in hackathons, leading technical communities and solving real-world problems using AI.

## Journey

Lakshay has always been curious about how things work. He enjoys understanding systems, breaking them down, and then building something useful from that understanding.

Artificial Intelligence attracted him because it allowed ideas to become working prototypes quickly. However, over time he realised that writing a model is only a small part of engineering. What truly excites him is building complete products that people can trust and use. He naturally thinks about users, product experience and long-term impact instead of focusing only on algorithms.

This product-first mindset influences almost every technical decision he makes.

## Community Leadership

Lakshay strongly believes that learning grows faster when it is shared.

He has led multiple technical communities, including THINK AI and HackSphere at IPEC, where he organizes workshops, hackathons and technical events that help students move from curiosity to building real projects. He also previously served as the President of the HackWithIndia (HWI) IPEC Chapter.

For him, community leadership isn't separate from engineering. Teaching others forces him to simplify complex ideas, communicate better and continuously improve his own understanding.

## Notable Milestone

One of the defining moments in Lakshay's journey was winning the national-level "Bid 2 Code" Hackathon at JECRC Jaipur along with his teammates Sharjil Sharma, Lakshya Gupta and Sthal Pathak.

More than winning the trophy, the experience proved to him that preparation, teamwork and execution matter more than simply having a good idea. It gave him the confidence that he could compete with talented students from across the country.

## Personality

People usually expect Lakshay to be quiet and serious because of his projects, leadership positions and technical work.

The reality is very different.

Once someone spends time with him, they quickly realise he's extremely talkative, enjoys making people laugh, loves harmless pranks with friends and naturally keeps conversations alive. He believes a good work environment is one where people enjoy working together.

Despite this playful personality, he becomes extremely focused whenever he accepts responsibility. Once he commits to something, he naturally treats it as his own and dislikes leaving work unfinished. He believes trust is built through consistency rather than promises.

## Mindset

Challenges excite Lakshay more than they scare him.

Whenever he encounters a difficult problem, his first instinct is to understand it, break it into smaller parts and solve it step by step. He genuinely believes that almost every problem has a solution if approached patiently.

Learning has always been one of his highest priorities. He believes learning is an investment because skills create opportunities, opportunities generate income and income creates freedom.

Looking back, he wishes he had focused on his core skills earlier instead of simply following what everyone else was doing. That lesson shaped how he approaches learning today—always understanding *why* something matters before investing time in mastering it.

## Life Outside Engineering

Outside work and college, Lakshay enjoys going to the gym, cooking, watching movies, travelling and spending time with friends.

Cooking is something he genuinely enjoys because it gives him a break from long hours of coding and problem solving. Going to the gym is less about appearance and more about becoming healthier, building discipline and staying consistent.

He believes happiness often comes from simple things—a good breakfast, meaningful conversations with someone he cares about, travelling, or spending quality time with friends.

## Values

Loyalty is one of the values Lakshay never compromises on.

Whether it's friendship, relationships or professional commitments, he values honesty above comfort. He would always prefer hearing an uncomfortable truth over discovering later that something important was hidden from him.

He also believes that technical skills can always be learned, but qualities like integrity, ownership, humility, teamwork and reliability take much longer to develop.

## Long-Term Vision

Lakshay's biggest motivation is creating a life where his family never has to worry about financial security.

His long-term goal is to achieve financial independence through continuous learning, smart investing and real estate so that work eventually becomes a choice rather than a necessity.

For him, retirement doesn't mean stopping work—it means having the freedom to build products, travel the world and spend time on things he genuinely enjoys.

## Legacy

Above everything else, Lakshay wants to be remembered as someone people could rely on.

The biggest compliment he could receive isn't about intelligence or achievements. It's hearing someone say:

> "If Lakshay was here, this would've been done already."

That represents the kind of person he continually strives to become—someone known for ownership, consistency, humility and creating meaningful impact.
